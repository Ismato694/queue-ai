## The one-pager (for a hospital owner/MD)

### Headline
**Give your patients their time back — and recover the revenue your queue is quietly losing.**

### The problem
Patients abandon long, unpredictable queues. You can't even see it happening — and every walk-out is lost consultation + lab + pharmacy revenue, plus a reputation hit.

### What Queue.ai does
Turns the hospital visit into a **live, predictable journey**:
- Patients know where they are, when to arrive, and when it's their turn (QR / web / SMS).
- Reception and staff get a one-tap "who's next."
- Managers see bottlenecks **before** they form and act in one click.
- Everyone sees one number: **Hours Returned**.

### Why it's different (not a queue app)
- **Removes decisions**, doesn't just show data.
- **Honest predictions** — every ETA shows confidence + the reasons (Trust Engine).
- **Industry-agnostic Flow Builder** — your exact workflow, no code.
- Built for Nigeria: works on cheap phones, SMS, and through network/power blips.

## FAQ

**Is this just a ticketing/queue system?**
No — it predicts and guides. It tells patients when to leave home, staff who's next, and managers what to fix, and it measures the hours saved.

**Do we need to replace our HMS/EMR?**
No. Queue.ai runs standalone; HL7/FHIR integration is available later if you want it.

**What about patients without smartphones?**
First-class: walk-in registration + SMS updates. No app required.

**Is patient data safe? (NDPR)**
Yes — encryption, tenant isolation, audit logs, names never shown on public screens, data hosted in-region. The hospital is the data controller; we sign a DPA.

**What if the internet or power fails?**
The reception desk keeps working offline (cached) with SMS fallback and a paper procedure; it reconciles when back online.

**Will our staff actually use it?**
Reception is faster than the whiteboard by lunch (<15s to add a walk-in); doctors get a one-tap screen; we train on-site and iterate.

**How do you charge?**
Monthly per branch (₦), plus metered SMS/WhatsApp. Pilot onboarding is free.

**How fast can we start?**
Live in one day; the free 2-week baseline begins immediately.

**How do you prove ROI?**
Baseline-capture mode records your "before"; the dashboard shows Hours Returned and Flow Score live — your own numbers, not our slides.
